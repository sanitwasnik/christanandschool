Messages from our administratives

Dear Parents and students,

It is with great delight that I welcome you to our school website. It is my pleasure and honour to be working with a school that inherits a rich legacy. I am truly privileged to be a part of this revered institution. This temple of wisdom and knowledge is a unique one. The success of education hinges on the collaborative efforts of people who care about the next generation. So I am excited to work with our outstanding Management, staff, students and parents to provide the best educational ambience possible for our students. It is our collective efforts that makes this institution such a vibrant place to learn, to work and explore. The provision of extracurricular activities like sports and cultural activities strengthen the process of learning and help to instill in the students the values of hard work, discipline, fair play and the spirit of sportsmanship. It is a well acknowledged and established fact that the Christanand School & Jr. College practice and promote a “holistic education” which is a remarkably rich blend of curricular, co-curricular and extracurricular learning and activities.

I encourage you to take the time to browse our website for more information.

I wish and pray that this will help to ensure the integral formation of our children.

Sr. Deepa Jose FCC

(Principal)

Dear Everyone,

It is generally believed that education is base to all developments. This is nothing but the all-round development of the individual’s personality. Education is necessary for all. Education makes the man what she or he should become. School is a platform where a child learns to speak, listen and behave in a befitting manner. School is the only place from where he unfolds the potentialities to become future artists, poets, writer’s scientists, leaders, doctors etc.

Christanand School is with the vision and mission to provide an excellent opportunity for the parents who look for the holistic development of their kids. With the long years of experience in the field of education, our educational organization has equipped with efficient faculties and improved facilities to make the ward ever competitive in all spheres of life.

So I welcome you all to this world of Knowledge.

Fr. Joseph Kalathil

(Manager)
